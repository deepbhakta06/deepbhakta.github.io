About Me

Hello, my name is Deep Bhakta, and I am a Computer Science major based in Los Angeles, California. Originally from India, I am the first in my family to attend college, which has taught me the value of hard work and perseverance. Currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science from California State University - Los Angeles, I am passionate about technology and all that it has to offer. In addition to my academic pursuits, I enjoy singing, watching movies, and playing sports such as volleyball, badminton, and tennis. Aside from my hobbies, I am also deeply involved in various side activities such as credit mentoring, stock trading, and freelance work. These experiences have allowed me to expand my skill set and hone my abilities outside of the classroom. As a first-generation student, I take pride in breaking barriers and paving the way for future generations. I believe that with hard work and dedication, anything is possible, and I am committed to making a positive impact in the world. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I look forward to exploring new opportunities and collaborations in the future.
